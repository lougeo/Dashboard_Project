DEMO: Laboratory Information Management System


Summary

This is a demo version for a database system designed for civil engineering labs, but could be adapted to any other industry which involves a similar process.

The process constitutes:
  • Technicians entering data.
  • Approval of this data by managers.
  • Generation of plots from this data.
  • Generation of PDF reports.
  • Automating delivery of reports to clients.
  • Giving clients on demand access to their reports.
Several points worth noting:
  • Because this is a demo version, some features, such as password reset, have been disabled.
  • This is a live, and public demo, and although I will be maintaining it regularily, please do not:
    • Use personal information
    • Use profanity
  • This is just a starting point, and the system is fully custumizable to any other potential requirements.
If you come across any bugs I would greatly appreciate you reaching out and letting me know!
Please do not hesitate to reach out for any other reason!
